Supergiant Kubernetes as a Service. Supported.

Supergiant is a supported auto-scaling cloud service that just works. We’ve made Kubernetes auto-healing infrastructure and cloud portability accessible to developers who want to ship a scalable service or app.

Unobstructed Kubernetes in the Cloud

Enjoy Kubernetes' self-healing infrastructure that just works at any scale, designed by engineers at Google to run billions of containers a week.

On-Demand Autoscaling on Any Cloud Provider

Automatically scale VMs and hardware to stay at > 80% utilization on AWS, Packet.net, DigitalOcean, OpenStack, and GCE, with more providers on the way.

Launch Services and Stacks Instantly From The Apps Marketplace

Push a button to launch cloud services like PostgreSQL, Nginx, Redis, and Jenkins, or add your own Helm chart repos to be included in your own personalized marketplace.

Everything You Can Do in the UI is Available in the API

Build with tools that support your workflow as you expand and grow. Let Supergiant support your developers who just want to ship apps from development to production.

100% Open Source

Supergiant is open-source under an Apache 2.0 license. We would love your feedback to help make Supergiant even better.

Awesome 24/7 Support. Really.

Supergiant is built and supported by the team behind Qbox.io -- the same team trusted by hundreds of organizations to deliver timely production support all over the world. We’ve got your back.
